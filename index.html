    About

    I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Virginia, now working with Prof. Jundong Li. Prior to joining in UVa, I obtained my bachelor’s degree from the School of Information and Communication Engineering at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), where I worked with Prof. Yingxia Shao and Prof. Wensheng Sun. (Last updated Aug. 2021)

    About

    I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of Georgia, now working with Prof. Ninghao Liu. I obtained my bachelor’s degree from the School of Statistic and Information at Shanghai University of International Business and Economics (SUIBE), where I worked with Prof. Chengcheng Hao and Prof. Liangliang Liu.

    Research Interests

    Natural Language Understanding: sentence representation learning, text structuring and knowledge extraction.

    Data Mining: recommender system, knowledge graph, multi-modals modelling.
